About Us

Our Journey

2016

The Idea

This idea comes to me and my friends when we are enjoying our tea at the evening of 24th October, 2016. We wanted to create a restaurant that would offer its customers their favorite fast food dishes - burgers, pizzas, and more.

2017

Opening

After making necessary preparations, completing our staff with experienced chefs and waiters, we were ready to meet new guests and visitors

2020

Fast Food Hub Today

We are devoted to our customers and to buisness of our life - offering fast food dishes. And that’s why today we’re recognized as #1 Fast Food Chain in PAKISTAN.

WHY US

We offer you not only the best recipes of typical dishes also the mood of many things: interior, music and waitresse. We put our hearts and souls into every dish we cook. Choose any dish from the menu which can help you to experience the real taste burger.

A lot of people admire our food. Big lovers of fast-food, pizza, burger, different kinds of sandwiches come to our burger club. Our menu provides you with the mix of fresh seasonal ingredients, refined goods and the best recipes and techniques.

No less important than a good cook is a good waiter. As clear as crystal black and white uniform, nice smile, and pleasant manners. Well-trained staff knows exactly how to serve the best visitors and how to impress them.
